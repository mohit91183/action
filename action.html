FREE FIRE

Free Fire is a mobile battle royale game where up to 50 players compete in fast-paced, 10-minute matches. Players scavenge for weapons, resources, and battle in a shrinking play area to be the last person or team alive.

BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) is a popular mobile battle royale game where players drop onto an island, scavenge for weapons, and fight in intense, strategic matches. Developed by Krafton, it offers a vibrant, engaging experience tailored for Indian players.

Call of Duty is a renowned first-person shooter series featuring intense combat scenarios across various historical and modern settings. Known for its gripping single-player campaigns and robust multiplayer modes, it offers immersive gameplay and high-quality graphics on multiple platforms.
